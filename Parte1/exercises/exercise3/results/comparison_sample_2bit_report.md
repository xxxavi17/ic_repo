## Definitions

- **MSE (L2 norm)**: Mean Squared Error - average of squared differences
- **Max Abs Error (L∞ norm)**: Maximum absolute difference between samples
- **SNR**: Signal-to-Noise Ratio in dB - 10 * log10(signal_power / noise_power)
